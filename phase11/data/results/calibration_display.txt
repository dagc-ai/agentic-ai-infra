The chunk travels through the ring until it reaches the root node, which receives the final chunk. The root then broadcasts its own chunk back through the ring. This pattern repeats until all N-1 steps complete. Every GPU sends and receives exactly N-1 chunks, each of size tensor_size / N. The algorithm is bandwidth-optimal — it uses the full ring bandwidth for every chunk.
